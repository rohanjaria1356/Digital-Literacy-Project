# Task 1 — Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Tool Used
**Canva** (canva.com) — a free, browser-based graphic design platform ideal for creating professional infographics without prior design experience.

## Infographic Overview

The infographic titled **"Digital Literacy 101: Your Essential Guide"** is a one-page visual resource designed to help first-year batchmates understand what digital literacy means and why it matters. It covers three core topics:

1. **What is Digital Literacy?** — A clear definition explaining that digital literacy goes beyond just using a computer; it encompasses the ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies responsibly and effectively.

2. **Essential Digital Tools for Students** — A curated list of free tools every student should know: GitHub for version control and collaboration, Google Workspace for cloud-based productivity, Canva for visual design, and LinkedIn for professional networking.

3. **Safe Internet Practices** — Key guidelines for staying safe online, including using strong and unique passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, recognising phishing attempts, and being mindful of the digital footprint you leave behind.

## Design Choices
The infographic uses a clean, modern layout with a blue-and-white colour palette to ensure readability. Icons and visual groupings are used to break down complex ideas into digestible sections, making it easy for students to scan and retain the information quickly.
