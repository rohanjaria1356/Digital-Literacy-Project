## Module Summaries

### Module 1 — Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page infographic using **Canva** covering what digital literacy means, useful digital tools for students, and safe internet practices. The infographic is designed to help batchmates quickly grasp why digital literacy matters in today's academic and professional world.

### Module 2 — Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Stack Overflow** — the three platforms most relevant to a CSE student. Each profile includes academic details and is designed to grow over the next four years as a living professional portfolio.

### Module 3 — Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner-level coding challenge on **HackerRank** and built a 5-question **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using **Google Forms**, exploring how cloud collaboration tools can enhance peer learning.

### Module 4 — Professional Email & Etiquette
Drafted two professional emails demonstrating proper subject lines, greetings, structure, and sign-offs. Also created a **Social Media Do's and Don'ts** checklist to promote responsible online behaviour among college students.

### Module 5 — Cybercrime Awareness
Researched **UPI/Online Payment Fraud** as a case study and created a comprehensive **Stay Safe Online** prevention checklist with 10 actionable tips tailored for Indian college students, including emergency reporting channels.
